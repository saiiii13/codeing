Write a function called inventory_tracker(items, new_item, new_qty, sell_item, sell_qty).
items is a dictionary of shop items and quantities.
new_item and new_qty are the item/quantity to add.
sell_item and sell_qty are the item/quantity to sell.
Your function should:
Add the new item (or increase it if it already exists).
Sell the given quantity from sell_item.
If sell_item does not exist, print "Item not found".
If trying to sell more than available, print "Not enough stock" and do not change it.
Print all items and quantities.
Return the updated dictionary and total stock count.
